Drayton Manor

Drayton Manor Theme Park is located in Tamworth, Staffordshire, in the heart of England. It is one of the most popular theme parks in the United Kingdom, and is home to some of the most thrilling rides in the country.

Rides

Drayton Manor Theme Park has something for everyone. From thrilling roller coasters to family-friendly rides, there is something for everyone. Here are a few of the most popular rides:

  • G-Force - A thrilling rollercoaster that features a vertical lift hill, a high-speed loop and a thrilling double-helix finale.
  • Apocalypse - The world's first and only stand-up stand-down roller coaster, with a vertical lift hill, two inversions and a thrilling double-helix finale.
  • Stormforce 10 - A high-speed water ride that features an intense drop and a hair-raising twisty section.
  • Shockwave - A suspended roller coaster that features a vertical lift hill, a 360 degree loop, a zero-G roll and a thrilling double-helix finale.
  • Maelstrom - A thrilling spinning boat ride that takes riders on a wild journey through a mysterious temple.
  • Maelstrom: The Dark Ride - A thrilling dark ride that takes riders on an epic adventure through a mysterious temple.
  • The Haunting - An indoor roller coaster and dark ride that takes riders on a thrilling journey through a haunted castle.
  • Hex - An indoor roller coaster and dark ride that takes riders on a mysterious journey through a cursed village.
  • Pandemonium - A thrilling spinning roller coaster that takes riders on a wild journey through a magical kingdom.
  • The Carousel - An old-fashioned carousel ride featuring wooden horses and brightly-colored chariots.
