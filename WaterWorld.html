Water World

Welcome to Water World!

Water World is an aquatic-themed amusement park located in the heart of the city. Our attractions include a giant water slide, lazy river, wave pool, and more! What are you waiting for? ome and join in for a fun and wet day!

  • Thunderbolt - Our most iconic and by far the most exhilarating ride yet, Thunderbolt is the UK’s first ever trap door drop water flume which sees you literally dropped into a 25mph water flume. This ride is not for the faint hearted. Dare you take the plunge?
  • Stormchaser - Hold tight stormchasers, you’re in for an adventure! Journey through Stormchaser, our longest ride throughout the whole Aqua Park. From dark black holes, to a colour show of flashing lights, the loops of Stormchaser is sure to have you in a spin.
  • Cyclone - Cyclone… One of most thrilling and magnificent attractions yet! embrace the force of nature as your rubber ring is propelled into a shuttle of turbulent water, before shooting out into an inward spiralling dome. At a staggering 19mph Cyclone is one of the fastest rides we have to offer. This is not a ride to be missed!
  • Nucleus - Waterworld’s first Water Rollercoaster blasts you along the slide in a rubber ring for 375 feet of ups and downs and is still a favourite with customers today.
  • Hurricane - Are you ready to feel the force of Hurricane as you descend at a speed of 17mph? With its “in slide” lighting effects and breath taking speed Hurricane is sure to get your adrenaline pumping!
