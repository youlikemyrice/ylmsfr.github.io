Welcome to Alton Towers

Alton Towers is the UK's premier theme park. With over 50 rides, attractions and live shows, it's a fun day out for all the family.

Our Attractions

  • Oblivion - The world's first vertical drop rollercoaster
  • Thirteen - An intense and thrilling ride through the dark
  • Galactica - A virtual reality rollercoaster experience
  • Nemesis - An iconic inverted rollercoaster
  • Curse of the Malta Cannons - A classic pirate boat ride

Live Shows

  • The Alton Towers Monster Show
  • Pirates of Mutiny Bay
  • Alton Towers Circus

Special Events

Alton Towers is a popular UK theme park, renowned for its thrilling rides and attractions. As well as regular days out, the park also hosts a variety of events throughout the year. These events range from special park-wide events such as Halloween Fright Nights and Christmas celebrations, to music festivals and live shows. Alton Towers also plays host to a number of corporate events, enabling businesses to make the most of the park’s attractions and facilities. With its wide range of attractions and events, Alton Towers is the perfect place for a day out or a special occasion.
